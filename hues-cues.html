Hues & Cues

Players (2–8)
Rounds per Player
rounds as Clue-Giver each
How to Play
The Clue-Giver sees a secret colour and gives one word. Other players tap their best guess on the colour grid. If no one is close, the Clue-Giver may give a second clue (two words).

3 pts — exact cell  |  2 pts — adjacent ring  |  1 pt — next ring out
Clue-Giver earns 1pt for each player scoring 3 or 2 pts.
Everyone else look away while the Clue-Giver sees the target colour.
