Game Notes

Build clear buffer spaces before you commit to long transfer chains.

How to Play

  • Choose a source glass. Its top color becomes the active water layer.
  • Choose a destination glass. A valid pour moves the top layer into any glass that has room.
  • Any glass accepts any color, as long as it isn't full.
  • You win when every non-empty glass is full and contains only one color.

Difficulty Structure

Easy uses 5 colors, medium uses 7, and hard uses 9. Every puzzle keeps two empty reserve glasses, so the tactical burden comes from sequence planning rather than brute scarcity.

Strategic Advice

Do not chase the first visible partial match. The stronger line is usually to free a buried color, consolidate reserve space, and only then lock a full monochrome glass.
