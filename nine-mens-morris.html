Rules of Play

Placement: players alternate placing stones on empty points until all eighteen are committed.

Mill: three of your stones in a straight line form a mill and immediately let you remove one opposing stone.

Movement: once reserves are gone, move one stone to an adjacent point.

Flying: when reduced to three stones, you may leap to any empty point.

Victory: reduce the opponent to two stones, or leave them with no legal move.
