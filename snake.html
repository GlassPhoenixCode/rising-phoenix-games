How to Play

Guide the serpent across the board, consume the glowing relic, and keep clear of the arena walls and your own body. Each relic extends the serpent by one segment.

  • Win condition: reach 12 relics and trigger the victory rite.
  • Loss condition: collide with a wall or your own coil.
  • Movement: use arrow keys, WASD, or the touch pad.
  • Pause: press P or use the pause button.
