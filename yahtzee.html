Rules Notes

Classic categories are implemented, including the upper-section bonus, large and small straights, full house, and Yahtzee.

Extra Yahtzees award a +100 bonus when your Yahtzee box already shows 50. Joker handling is also supported: if the matching upper box is open, it must be used; otherwise lower boxes become available with their fixed joker values.

Once all thirteen scoring categories are filled, the game ends, confetti bursts across the board, and your final total is preserved as a local best score if it exceeds your previous record.
