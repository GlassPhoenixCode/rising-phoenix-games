Field Notes

Desktop: left click reveals, right click flags. Touch: use mode buttons.

Each revealed square tells you how many mines touch it in the eight surrounding spaces. Use those counts to infer where the hidden charges lie and mark them before you open the rest of the field.

How to Play

  • Reveal a tile to inspect it. The first reveal is always safe.
  • Numbers show how many mines touch that tile, including diagonals.
  • Flags mark suspected mines. They do not win the game by themselves.
  • Win by revealing every safe tile. All unrevealed tiles left at that point are mines.
  • Tip: click a revealed number again to chord nearby tiles once you have placed the correct number of flags around it.

Legend

  • Reveal Mode: opens covered tiles.
  • Flag Mode: places or removes a flag on a covered tile.
  • Chord Reveal: on a revealed number, open all unflagged neighbors if the surrounding flag count matches the number.
