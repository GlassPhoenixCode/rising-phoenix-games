House Rules

  • Number cards count at face value. Face cards count as 10. Aces count as 11 unless that would bust the hand, then they count as 1.
  • Beat the dealer by finishing closer to 21 without going over. If both totals match, the hand is a push.
  • The dealer reveals the hidden card after you stand or bust, then draws until reaching 17 or higher.
  • A player win triggers the phoenix victory flourish across the table.
