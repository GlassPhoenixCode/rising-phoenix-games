Core Rules

  • Choose one pit on your side and sow its stones counterclockwise, one per pit.
  • If your final stone lands in your own store, you immediately take another turn.
  • If your final stone lands in an empty pit on your side, you capture that stone and the opposite pit.
  • When one side is emptied, the remaining stones on the other side are swept into that player's store.

Tactical Read

Tempo in Mancala comes from chaining extra turns while denying captures. A strong move is often the one that changes the next three turns, not merely the current count.
