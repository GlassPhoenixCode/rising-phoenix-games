How to Play

Match three or more bubbles of the same color to burst them. Any bubbles left hanging without a path to the ceiling will fall away. After several missed shots, the ceiling descends. Clear the entire field before the cluster reaches the danger line.

Row Surge Mode

Toggle Row Surge on to force a fresh row of random bubbles onto the ceiling after every 10 launched shots. Each surge pushes all remaining bubbles downward. If any bubble touches the danger line after the surge, the round ends immediately.

Controls

Aim: move the pointer or drag on the board.
Fire: click, tap, or release after dragging.
Wall banks: shots ricochet off both side walls.
