How to Play

Keep the ball in play and force it past the crimson paddle. The ball accelerates as rallies grow longer, and the angle of contact changes the rebound. The first side to reach seven points wins the match.

Controls

Mouse / TouchMove anywhere over the court to guide your paddle vertically.
KeyboardUse W / S or ↑ / ↓ for precise control.
Start / PauseUse the buttons below the court or press the space bar.
ResetBegin a fresh match with scores returned to zero.

Design Intent

This build follows the Rising Phoenix Games cabinet style: dark torchlit panels, gold-forward typography, a centered title block, and a hard-coded phoenix logo in the header. Victory triggers a burst of confetti and a central proclamation that expands across the court before fading.
