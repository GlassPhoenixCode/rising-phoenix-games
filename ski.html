Field Guide
A clean line matters more than reckless speed.
What to Avoid
🌲
Trees are lethal obstacles. Any solid collision ends the run instantly.
🪨
Rocks are narrower but less forgiving when they appear inside a tight lane.
⚑
There is no finish gate. Survive until you crash, then continue until all three crashes are spent.
How It Works
The slope scrolls continuously while your skier carves left and right. There is no maximum point total and no finish distance. You have three crashes per session, and the course speed increases by 0.25x every 1000 meters. Your best single-run distance is stored locally on this device.
Stay near the center until you read the lane pattern. Wide horizontal swings create unnecessary risk because the course keeps accelerating every 1000 meters.
