Game Guide
Mouse, touch, and drag swapping are enabled.

Rules

  • Swap one candy with an orthogonally adjacent candy.
  • Valid swaps must create a line of at least three matching candies.
  • Each valid swap consumes one move.
  • Reach the target score before moves run out to win.
  • L and T shapes create wrapped candies with explosive area clears.

Special Candies

4 in a row creates a striped candy that clears a full row or column.
5 in a row creates a color bomb that clears every candy of one color.
L and T matches create wrapped candies that detonate in a 3×3 burst.

How to Play

Click then click an adjacent candy, or drag one candy onto another. On touch screens, tap one candy and then an adjacent candy. Cascades increase your score rapidly, so do not chase only the first obvious match.

Scoring

  • Standard candy cleared: 60 points.
  • Striped candy activation: bonus 120 points per candy cleared in the blast.
  • Color bomb activation: bonus 180 points per candy cleared by the blast.
  • Wrapped candy detonation: bonus 140 points for each candy caught in the blast.
  • Cascades multiply match value by chain depth.
