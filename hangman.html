Game Guide
A classic word duel with a Rising Phoenix finish.
Category Pool
How It Works
Guess one letter at a time. Every incorrect guess adds another line to the hanging figure. Complete the full word before the sixth error and you win. Use Reveal a Letter sparingly—the hint exposes a hidden character but counts as a wrong step.
Keyboard Legend
  • A green key marks a correct letter.
  • A crimson key marks a failed guess.
  • Gold accents indicate live puzzle state and active category.
Victory Condition
Win by revealing every letter in the phrase. A confetti burst will crown the board, and a themed victory proclamation will swell across the puzzle before fading away.
Word Pools
The game includes mixed, fantasy, science, art, and myth word sets, each paired with a clue to keep guessing grounded rather than arbitrary.
