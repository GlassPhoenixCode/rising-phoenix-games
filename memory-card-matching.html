Rules & Notes
Match all hidden pairs before the deck yields completely.

This is a single-player concentration game. Turn over two cards at a time. If the glyphs match, the pair remains revealed. If they differ, both cards turn back over after a short pause.

  1. Choose a difficulty to set the board size and total number of pairs.
  2. Click or tap any concealed card to reveal it.
  3. Reveal a second card. Matching glyphs lock in place; nonmatching glyphs hide again.
  4. Your score is tracked primarily by move count. Lower is better.
  5. The brief reveal control exposes the full board for a moment, but it adds a move penalty.
