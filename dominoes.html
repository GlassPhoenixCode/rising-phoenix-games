Mode
Block dominoes with clean hotseat or solo play.

Round Rules

A double-six set is shuffled. Each side receives seven tiles. Match one open end of the table chain. If you cannot play, draw until you can or the boneyard empties. When both sides are stuck, the lower pip total wins the block.

Controls

Playable tiles glow. Click the left half of a tile to place it on the left end, or the right half to place it on the right end. In hotseat mode, both hands are visible so turns can alternate on one device.
