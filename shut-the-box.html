▶ ⚜ How to Play
🎲

The Goal

Nine numbered tiles — 1 through 9 — stand open at the start of every turn. Roll the dice and flip tiles down until you are stuck. The lower your final score the better. Flip every last tile and you Shut the Box — a perfect score of zero.

🔢

Rolling & Flipping

Each turn, roll both dice and add them together. Select any combination of still-open tiles whose values sum exactly to the dice total, then press Flip Tiles to close them. You may use any number of tiles — e.g. a roll of 8 could close the 8, or the 5+3, or the 6+2, or the 1+3+4, and so on.

🎯

Scoring Your Turn

After flipping, roll again and repeat. Your turn ends the moment no valid combination of open tiles matches the current roll. Your score is the sum of all tiles still standing. In multiplayer the player with the lowest score after everyone has had a turn wins.

🎲

The Single-Die Rule

When the total of all remaining open tiles is 6 or less, you may choose to roll only a single die before your next toss. A checkbox will appear automatically offering you this option — use it to avoid overshooting the small numbers that remain.

🏆

Multiplayer

Choose 2–4 players in the setup screen. Players take turns one at a time — each completes their full run before handing off. Press Next Player → to pass the box. The results table at the end ranks everyone by score; lowest wins. Ties share the victory.

🔥

Perfect Game

Shut every tile before your turn ends and you score a glorious zero. The box is sealed, the tavern erupts — a full celebration plays and your personal best is recorded forever. In multiplayer the other players still finish their turns; the lowest score still wins.

Quick Reference
Open tiles:1 – 9 (all face up)
Dice:Two d6 (one if remaining ≤ 6)
Valid flip:Any subset summing to the roll
Turn ends:No valid subset exists
Score:Sum of remaining open tiles
Perfect:Score 0 — Shut the Box!
