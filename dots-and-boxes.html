The Lattice Is Won

A master of lines has claimed the board.

How to Play
Close a square, keep the turn, outscore your rival.

Players alternate drawing one line per turn. Whenever a move completes a square, that square is claimed, a point is awarded, and the same player immediately takes another turn.

Core Rules

  • Click any glowing unclaimed edge between two dots.
  • If your line closes one or more boxes, those boxes become yours.
  • Closing a box grants an extra turn instead of passing play.
  • When every box is claimed, the higher score wins the match.

Tactical Notes

  • Early safe edges matter; avoid handing your opponent an easy fourth side.
  • Long chains become decisive near the endgame.
  • A move can capture multiple boxes at once if it closes several open cells.
