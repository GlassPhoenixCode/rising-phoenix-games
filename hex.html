Field Notes
Place one stone per turn. Crossings are impossible in Hex.

Victory Conditions

Crimson wins by linking the left edge to the right edge.
Azure wins by linking the top edge to the bottom edge.

How to Play

Choose any empty hex on your turn. Once placed, stones never move. The board can never end in a draw, so every complete game has exactly one victor.

Tactical Reminder

Central influence is powerful, but virtual connections and forcing responses matter more than surface symmetry. Think in linked groups rather than isolated stones.
