The Phoenix Ascends

You have forged the 2048 ember.

Chronicle

Slide the board with arrow keys, WASD, or a swipe gesture. Matching tiles merge into a stronger value. Every move spawns a new ember, so inefficient turns accumulate pressure quickly.

Goal
Create a 2048 tile
Undo

Practical Strategy

  • Anchor your largest tile in one corner.
  • Avoid moving against that corner unless recovery is obvious.
  • Prefer long chains along one edge instead of scattered merges.

Touch Support

On phones and tablets, drag across the puzzle in the direction you want the board to shift. The puzzle ignores very small gestures to reduce accidental moves.
